Rites of Ash and Frost

Five years ago, Dani Greenbottle left Barrow Hill in the middle of the night and never looked back. She left behind a life she couldn't fit into, a destiny she didn't want — and the one woman she never stopped loving.

Now a letter has found her on the road. It's from Evelynn Thistledown, priestess of the Eversong, and it says one thing: Come home.

Something is wrong. Midsummer has arrived, but winter hasn't left. Crops are dying. Wells are frozen. And Evie, steady, radiant, unshakeable Evie, is frightened.

Dani comes. Of course she comes.
